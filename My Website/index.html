Camping trip

My camping trip to High Falls State Park

By:Lucas Moore

Arrival

When my family and I finally arrived at High Falls State Park, we started to look for a decent camping spot. After a few minutes, we found a spot that was a little bit further from others but a bit further away from the waterfalls(not super far). We began to unpack everything we had brought.

  • Flashlights
  • 2 tents
  • Food and water
  • Radio
  • Blankets
  • Lantern
  • Sleeping bags

My sister and I went out to check the area around our camping site. We went to check out the waterfall that was close by. We watched the waterfall from afar for a little bit before heading back to our camping site. When we got back we went out to collect some wood for the fire with our mom while our dad gathered some rocks to make a little fire pit just to be a little bit careful.

  1. Gather Logs and sticks for fuel
  2. Create a small fire pit with gathered rocks
  3. Place Logs and sticks into a T-shape
  4. Use match/lighter to start the fire
