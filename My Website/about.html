Camping trip

What I saw and felt while at these locations

These are all gonna be short summaries of these pictures below please enjoy.

Figure 1

Waterfalls, Lakes, Mountains, and rivers anything like these quiet locations are my favorite. The peace and quiet is unlike any other and relaxes me. This is how I felt here calm, quiet, and happy. There wasn't many people around and that made my time here precious to me and I'll never forget it. The sun was begining to go down and the sound of the water falling onto the rocks below was amazing.
